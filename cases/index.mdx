This section contains practical scenarios and examples.

The goal is not to present polished success stories. The goal is to show how the stance behaves under real constraints.

## What belongs here

- legacy modernization inside active systems
- vendor-driven friction and ownership gaps
- platform or security seams slowing delivery
- cases where taper worked
- cases where the model should have said no

## How to read these cases

Each case should help answer:

- what the real constraint was
- where ownership lived
- what the first safe move was
- what anti-pattern had to be avoided
- what changed after the work
