# Building Capability Continuously

We prioritize increasing customer self-sufficiency over delivering impressive artifacts. Every interaction should transfer knowledge, develop skills, and strengthen organizational capability.

## Key Approaches

### Teaching Over Doing
- Explain techniques while demonstrating them
- Encourage customer team members to lead activities
- Provide guided practice opportunities rather than solo execution
- Create learning objectives for every engagement activity

### Gradual Ownership Transfer
- Start with collaborative approaches before transitioning to customer-led initiatives
- Establish clear capability milestones tied to project phases
- Plan explicit knowledge transfer checkpoints throughout engagements
- Validate customer readiness before reducing Paperclip involvement

### Institutional Learning
- Capture lessons learned in reusable formats
- Connect current work to broader organizational objectives
- Identify and develop customer champions for ongoing improvement
- Build feedback mechanisms into delivered solutions

## Implementation Techniques

1. **Paired Problem Solving**: Work alongside customer team members on real issues
2. **Train-the-Trainer Programs**: Equip customer team members to educate others
3. **Reference Materials**: Create documentation that supports ongoing learning
4. **Skill Gap Assessments**: Regularly evaluate and address capability deficiencies
5. **Community Building**: Foster peer networks for continued support

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

- Prioritizing speed over learning in delivery-focused engagements
- Creating "hero" dependencies on specific Paperclip personnel
- Delivering solutions without adequate training and transition planning
- Focusing on outputs rather than outcomes that increase customer capability
- Neglecting to validate that transferred knowledge is effectively applied
