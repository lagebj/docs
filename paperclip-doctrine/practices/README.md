# Paperclip Operational Practices

Our practices translate principles into concrete behaviors. They represent the day-to-day ways we bring our principles to life.

These practices are not rigid procedures but flexible approaches that guide our work while allowing for adaptation to specific contexts.

Each practice includes concrete examples and guidance for application in various scenarios.
