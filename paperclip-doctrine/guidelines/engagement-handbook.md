# Client Engagement Handbook

This handbook provides practical guidance for conducting client engagements aligned with Paperclip principles and practices.

## Engagement Phases

### Discovery and Planning
- Conduct thorough context analysis before proposing solutions
- Engage all relevant stakeholders in problem definition
- Document assumptions and constraints explicitly
- Establish clear success criteria tied to customer capability
- Plan for gradual ownership transfer from the beginning

### Implementation and Delivery
- Work collaboratively with customer teams rather than in isolation
- Explain techniques while demonstrating them
- Document decisions and rationale comprehensively
- Conduct regular check-ins on capability development
- Adapt approaches based on customer feedback

### Transition and Closure
- Validate customer readiness for independent operation
- Document systems and processes for ongoing maintenance
- Provide training and support materials for continued learning
- Establish feedback mechanisms for continuous improvement
- Plan explicit handoff procedures with clear ownership assignments

## Communication Guidelines

- Use plain language accessible to all stakeholders
- Explain the "why" behind recommendations, not just the "what"
- Make trade-offs explicit rather than implicit
- Provide regular progress updates proactively
- Document important conversations and decisions

## Quality Assurance

- Regular peer reviews of approach and progress
- Customer feedback collection at key milestones
- Risk assessment and mitigation throughout engagement
- Adherence to simplicity and reversibility principles
- Focus on capability building over artifact delivery

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

- Rushing into solutions without proper diagnosis
- Creating dependencies rather than building independence
- Focusing on impressive deliverables over meaningful impact
- Ignoring feedback or resistance from customer teams
- Completing engagements without validating sustainable outcomes
