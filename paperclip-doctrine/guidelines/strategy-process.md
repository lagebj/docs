# Strategy Development Process

This process guides the development of strategic initiatives that align with Paperclip principles and create genuine value for customers.

## Strategic Thinking Framework

### Problem Definition
- Begin with real customer challenges rather than assumed solutions
- Distinguish between symptoms and root causes
- Consider multiple perspectives and stakeholder needs
- Validate assumptions with evidence from actual contexts

### Option Generation
- Develop multiple approaches to every significant challenge
- Consider both incremental and transformative possibilities
- Evaluate options against our core principles explicitly
- Seek input from diverse viewpoints and experiences

### Decision Making
- Weight criteria based on customer outcomes and our values
- Consider long-term implications beyond immediate benefits
- Plan for realistic implementation constraints
- Establish clear success metrics and feedback mechanisms

## Implementation Planning

### Capability Alignment
- Ensure proposed strategies match customer organizational capacity
- Plan capability development alongside strategic initiatives
- Identify and address potential skill or knowledge gaps
- Create learning objectives for all strategic activities

### Risk Management
- Identify potential failure modes and mitigation strategies
- Plan for realistic resource requirements and constraints
- Establish monitoring mechanisms for early warning signs
- Create contingency plans for significant dependencies

### Success Measurement
- Define measurable outcomes tied to customer capability
- Establish regular review cycles with stakeholders
- Collect feedback from multiple perspectives
- Adjust approaches based on learning and results

## Anti-Patterns to Avoid

- Starting with solutions rather than problems
- Ignoring organizational dynamics and cultural factors
- Creating strategies that require unrealistic resources or changes
- Focusing on impressive visions rather than practical improvements
- Failing to plan for measurement and adjustment
