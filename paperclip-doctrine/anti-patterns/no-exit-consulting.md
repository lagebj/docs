# No-Exit Consulting

Creating arrangements where customer success depends on permanent Paperclip presence. This violates our core principle of building customer capability over dependency.

## Why It's Problematic

Dependency-based relationships undermine customer autonomy and our mission. They create financial incentives that conflict with customer best interests.

## Signs of No-Exit Consulting

- Customers unable to operate systems without Paperclip support
- Exclusive knowledge residing in Paperclip personnel
- Systems requiring specialized tools or processes Paperclip provides
- Pricing structures that disincentivize customer independence
- Lack of transition planning or capability development

## How to Avoid

- Plan for gradual ownership transfer from engagement start
- Document systems comprehensively for customer personnel
- Train customer teams on all aspects of implemented solutions
- Build systems that don't require specialized knowledge
- Structure engagements around capability milestones

## Recovery Strategies

- Audit current dependencies for customer independence
- Accelerate knowledge transfer and training programs
- Document informal processes and tribal knowledge
- Introduce standardized tools and practices
- Create explicit transition plans with timelines
