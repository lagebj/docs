## Overall Phase Success Criteria:

1. All design system documentation is complete and published
2. Contribution guidelines enable effective team collaboration

- Completed implementation from previous phases

- Stakeholder review and approval of all documentation
- Team feedback on contribution guidelines usability
- Training effectiveness assessment through knowledge checks
- Documentation completeness audit against requirements
- Adoption tracking through contribution metrics
